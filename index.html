Social Media Ethics

What

Netiquette, which comes from the words "net" and "etiquette," refers to internet guidelines that advise people on how to communicate and treat others when interacting online (Chen, n.d.).

Dont's

1. Send Spam

2. Post hate comments

3. Don't use sarcasm freely

4. Don't overuse the chat box

5. Leave information unverified

Why

Netiquette is important as it helps students maintain a positive and productive online learning environment. It also fosters respectful communication among students, instructors, and other people online (Potomac, July 16, 2024).

Do's

1. Use proper grammar

2. Be credible

3. Cite sources

4. Remember that nothing is private
online

5. Respect people’s privacy
